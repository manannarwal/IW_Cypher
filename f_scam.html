Financial Scams and Frauds

Frauds and Scams are problems of which thousands of people fall victims to. These scams can affect people of various age groups, that's why it's important to acknowledge.

How to

Avoid it?

  • Scams and Frauds are of various types, Phishing links, Calls, Credit Card manipulations and many more.
  • Suspicious link promoting unrealistic offers must be avoided, they may potentially be a malware to get a hold of your personal information
  • Using strong passwords, it is a commonly used statement but holds great value. Your passwords must be strong which makes them hard to crack.
  • Some common tactics used by scammers are "Sense of Urgency", they often make you feel like you have to act immediately, but you must carefully analyse the situation and act accordingly.
  • Keep your softwares up to date this keeps them secure.

    • --What to do if you are already

    a Victim . .

  • Stop any further interaction with the one responsible.
  • Secure your accounts. Cease all online transactions and if you notice deductions in balance, report immediately.
  • File a report of the authorities. Inform your bank about the same they'll stop any transactions from the scammer's side.
  • Panicking in such a situation is not helpful, get help from the officials under such cases.
